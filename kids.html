Rabbits & Wolves!

Discover how nature keeps everything in balance! Learn why 🐰 rabbits and 🐺 wolves need each other using a super cool math trick called the Lotka-Volterra equations!

🌳 Once Upon a Time in a Forest... The Story

Imagine a beautiful forest full of cute 🐰 rabbits and sneaky 🐺 wolves. When there are lots of rabbits hopping around, the wolves have plenty of yummy food, so the wolf families grow bigger! 🐺👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

But as more wolves hunt, the rabbit numbers go down. 📉 With fewer rabbits to munch on, the wolves get hungry and their numbers drop too. With fewer wolves around, the rabbits can safely have baby bunnies again! 🐰✨ And the whole circle starts over!

💡 Cool Fact!

The 🐺 wolves always trail behind the 🐰 rabbits! When rabbits are everywhere, wolves grow — but by the time the wolves peak, rabbits have already started disappearing! It's like a never-ending game of follow-the-leader! 🏃‍♂️

How good wolves are at catching rabbits. Super skilled hunters catch more! 🎯

🐺 γ Wolf Hunger Rate

How quickly wolves get hungry without food. Hungry wolves can't survive long! 😰

🐺 δ Food → Babies

How well wolves turn their meals into baby wolf pups! Nom nom → 🐺 puppy! 🍽️➡️🐺

🔄 The Circle of Life! How It Works

  • Phase 1 🐰📈: Lots of rabbits hopping around → wolves eat well → wolf families grow bigger!
  • Phase 2 🐺📈: Too many wolves! → rabbits getting caught → rabbit numbers crash! 😱
  • Phase 3 📉📉: Not enough rabbits left → wolves go hungry → wolf numbers drop too
  • Phase 4 🐰✨: Few wolves around → rabbits bounce back → the cycle starts again! 🎉
